Sài Gòn Hà Nội Đà Nẵng Hạ Long

Non Sông is a souvenir brand that tells the story of Vietnam through the frame of an airplane window — where everyday experiences become lasting memories. Across Hà Nội, Sài Gòn, Đà Nẵng, and Hạ Long, each collection captures a unique perspective.

Collection 01

Sài Gòn

A glimpse of Sài Gòn through everyday icons — movement, street food, and familiar city scenes. Designed as simple, usable objects that carry the rhythm of the city with you.

Collection 02

Hà Nội

A quieter rhythm of Hà Nội — coffee, streets, and still moments by the lake. Designed as simple objects that carry the calm and depth of the city.

Collection 03

Đà Nẵng

A lighter side of Đà Nẵng — sea, sun, and open spaces. Designed as simple objects that carry a sense of ease and freedom.

Collection 04

Hạ Long

A quieter view of Hạ Long — water, limestone, and stillness. Designed as simple objects that carry a sense of calm and space.
